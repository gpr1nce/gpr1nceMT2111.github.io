Introduction

I am not a new developer, but a good bootcamp has me thinking in new ways.

Currently in MicroTrain's Full Stack Web / Hybrid Mobile App Dev course, I've been astonished at how powerful client side tools have become since I studied HTML v2.

Imagine coming home after a long day on the job building buttons for websites, parking your Saturn, and waking up the next morning with a Tesla in the driveway.

Today, ambition in Web design can vault over technical debt to work with effects that are routinely made available to anyone bringing a Web page to life. From browser dev tools to style and menu generators there are great resources available.

It's a design world both more demanding and more generous than ever I imagined.
